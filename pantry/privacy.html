Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pantry, operated by Shreya Rathi. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Pantry app.

2. Information We Collect

When you sign up for Pantry, we collect the following information:

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Pantry items, shopping lists, and food consumption diary entries you choose to save

We use Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore (database) to securely store and manage your data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Save your pantry inventory, shopping lists, and food diary entries
  • Improve the features and functionality of the Pantry app

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only accessible by you through your authenticated account. We may share data only when required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Security

We use Firebase's secure infrastructure to store your data safely. While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You can access, update, or delete your information at any time through the Pantry app. If you would like to delete your account or request more information about your data, please contact us at rathishreya2005@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Us
